Dear Respondents,

Greetings of Peace! The researchers respectfully invite participation in the research study entitled "Optimizing Travel and Tour Agency Operations with Microsoft Excel: A Comparative Analysis of Manual and Automated Processes in Puerto Princesa City" as a partial requirement in Accounting Research Methods. The purpose of this study is to assess and compare the operational efficiency of travel and tour agencies in Puerto Princesa City that use either manual processes or Excel-based automated systems. The goal is to evaluate the impact of Microsoft Excel automation on workflow efficiency, data accuracy, staff productivity, and overall operational performance.

Respondents are kindly asked to allocate 15-30 minutes to compete a structured questionnaire. By participating in the study, the respondents will be able to explain the evolving communication patterns in which they partake in contemporary society especially in the social media context. Additionally, all information gathered from this survey including the respondent's identity will be kept anonymize and confidential, and will only be accessed by authorized personnel. This confidentiality and anonymity of data will be employed to the extent allowed by the law, Data Privacy Act of 2012 (R.A. 10173) which aims to protect individuals' personal information. On the other hand, any information obtained from the respondents will be used for academic and research purposes only.

Participation in this study is completely voluntary. For instance, if there will come a time that respondent will feel uncomfortable participating in the survey, unquestionably they have the right to withdraw without facing any consequences.
